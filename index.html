Who Am I

A campaigner.An artist.

When I was a kid, I used to watch this TV ad-commercial saying, “ Essel world mei rahunga mei, ghar nahi nahi nahi jaunga mei” meaning “I’ll stay in Essel world, I don’t want to go home”! Believe me, I am feeling the same on my last day at IHS-Markit.

4 years at IHS-Markit changed me from a carefree ‘fresher’ from Delhi Technological University to a responsible ‘Software Developer’. It’s because of you all.

You helped me to hone my technical skills and supported me in my professional and personal life. It was more like working with my family than colleagues. Be it CSR forums, sports events, fun committee events, pool room, Table tennis or IBOXX as a unit, I made zillion friends here!

I would like to thank Mr. Amit Vasdev, Mr. Shalabh Agrawal and Mr. Jogendra Singh for their guidance and support.

I will now start my journey again as a student at Texas A&M University (Masters-Management Information Systems) and I hope I’ll be back to work with this amazing organization!
